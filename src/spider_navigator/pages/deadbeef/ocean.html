Have you ever wondered what it's like to not think anymore? To be ripped from your mind and placed somewhere else?

I know. I am at the very least, in control for now. But my consciousness lapses. I can only write my pain. And so you shall know it too.

I remember I was a writer. Or at least, a semblance of one. I had realized instead I could make a larger sum of funds by smuggling goods through the blockade. The British caught us almost 4 times. But our ship was too quick each time they had spotted us.

It was the 5th time that had killed us. We had merely gotten unlucky, I suppose. The fog was too thick to see across the English Channel, and we could not be sure of our heading. I was in my quarters when I had heard my first mate scream.

"They are upon us!"

I lept to my feet and rushed on to the deck. I saw the man of war off our starboard bow. We were sailing directly into their cannons.

It didn't take them long to fire. I was already running for the rowboat while the cannons tore through our hull. And in the flicker of a flame upon a candle's wick, it was over. Ours was only meant for cargo. It was crippled in an instant.

They made use of rope and chain to press alongside us. The English sent soldiers to board us. We were all rounded up, brought aboard their ship. I could only watch as my ship sank into the channel. We were pressed into the service at gunpoint.

Except for me. The commander looked at me with such contempt as I had never seen.

"You will be our example. Let there be no smuggling in these waters."

I was determined not to let my life go to waste. I ran towards the man and stretched my hands around his neck. At once, one of the soldiers cut through my side. I felt the blood rush from me and fell to the deck. I must have let out a great scream, for the commander of the English ship said:

"For that, I will make your screams the only example your men will ever need."

It was then that he took me below deck, and my pains were the nourishment for any would-be deserters as the rest sailed into harbor.

As for me? My name was Alexander Jacob.

I have been murdered.
